Name in Chinese： 中国科学院杭州医学研究所
Name in English: Hangzhou Institute of Medicine, Chinese Academy of Science.
URL to institute website in chinese: https://him.cas.cn/ 
URL to institute website in english: https://english.him.cas.cn/

The institute belong to Chinese Academy of Science system and therefore trainning Master and PhD students from University of Chinese Academy of Science.
The institute has an affiliated hospital called Zhejiang Cancer Hospital, URL: https://www.zchospital.com/
